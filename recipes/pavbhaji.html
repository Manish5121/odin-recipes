Pav bhaji

Description

Pav Bhaji is a fast food dish from Mumbai (Bombay), India, consisting of a vegetable curry (bhaji) cooked in tomato gravy and served with a soft bread roll (pav). While Bhaji is a traditional Indian name for a vegetable dish, the Pav or Pao was the Portuguese word for bread, which was introduced by them during their brief presence in Mumbai around the mid-1500s.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Chopped Cup(78.0 gm) Tomato
  • 1/4 Chopped Cup(28.0 gm) Onion
  • 1/4 Boiled Diced Cup(45.0 gm) Potato
  • 1/4 Chopped Cup(26.0 gm) Bottle Gourd
  • 1/4 Chopped Cup(26.0 gm) Cauliflower
  • 1/4 Chopped Cup(36.0 gm) Capsicum
  • 1/4 Boiled Cup(39.0 gm) Peas
  • 1.0 Chopped Tbsp(3.0 gm) Coriander Leaves
  • 2.0 Tbsp(13.0 gm) Pav Bhaji Masala
  • 1.0 Powder Tsp(2.0 gm) Red Chilly
  • 1.0 Tsp(4.0 gm) Butter
  • 1.0 Tsp(2.0 gm) Dhania Powder
  • 1/2 Tsp(3.0 gm) Ginger Garlic Paste
  • 1/2 Tsp(1.0 gm) Haldi and 1/4 Tsp(0.59 gm) Jeera
  • 1/4 Tsp(0.53 gm) Dry Mango Powder
  • 1/2 Tsp(2.0 gm) Salt and 1.0 Tsp(3.0 ml) Oil
  • 200.0 Ml(200.0 ml) Water
  • 6.0 Number(286.0 gm) Pav

Steps

  1. Heat a pan, add 1 tsp oil, 1/4 tsp jeera, 1/2 tsp ginger garlic paste, 1/4 cup chopped onion and saute it well.
  2. Then add 1/4 tsp salt, 1/4 cup chopped bottle gourd, 2 tbsp boiled peas, 3 tbsp chopped cauliflower, water as required, 1/2 tsp haldi, saute them well.
  3. Then add 1/4 cup boiled and diced potato, saute well. Add water as required and cover with lid & cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Remove the lid, mash the bhaji with a masher
  5. Add 1/2 cup chopped tomato, 1 tbsp pav bhaji masala, 1 tbsp red chilli powder, 1 tsp dhania powder, 1/4 tsp dhania powder, 1/4 tsp amchur powder, 1 tsp butter, mix and mash well.
  6. Then Add 2 tbsp chopped capsicum, mix and mash it well. Cover with lid, cook for 5 minutes. Mash them well again.
  7. Garnish it with 1 tbsp chopped coriander leaves, mix it well.
  8. Serve it hot with pav and chopped onions
